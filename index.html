Homepage - Yufei Zhang

Hello there! I am an assistant professor at Department of Statistics of London School of Economics and Political Science since September 2021. Before that, I did my PhD in the Mathematical Institute at University of Oxford under the supervision of Christoph Reisinger.  My thesis won the Mathematical Institute DPhil Thesis Prize 2021.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, stochastic control and games, and mathematical finance. Advanced machine learning techniques lead to fast algorithms for analysing and controlling complex stochastic systems in data-rich environments, while tools from stochastic control theory reveal the principles behind machine learning techniques. My works combine stochastic control theory and statistical learning techniques in a novel way. They provide a theoretically-grounded framework to systematically design safe and efficient algorithms for high-stake decision making in finance and engineering.

Please have a look at Research and CV for more details. I'm always happy to discuss about any of these topics for projects and collaboration, so please feel free to get in touch!
